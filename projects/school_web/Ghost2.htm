Pursue the Lords of the Sea!

Everyone knows that the core of a Ghost hunt is the realism of the stories and how they
immerse you in the frightenning fights and daring escapades of the very ghosts you almost
know. In this aspect, the only true guide to choose is Lord Flameheart, Captian of the
Burning Blade.
He doesn't just recite the old tales as many do, he creates the tales around you with powers
beyond the mortal realm. As he sails you around the Sea of Thieves, battling Skeleton Lords
such as Greymarrow and even the Pirate Lord, Flameheart teaches you all about the history of
the Sea of Thieves with ghostly tales of murder, betrayal and riches Galore!
As Sir Arthur Pendragon said after the experience, "The weak-minded collapse into madness at
this display of horror and gore"

Lords Online!

Spectral glimpeses into the Sea of the Dammed aren't uncommon upon the Burning Blade. A
Spanish Fort under seige from Davy Jones and the black pearl, a small view into Old Sailors Isle.
As the tales go on, the more frequent they are, until, at last, you're on the very decks of the
Flying Dutchman.
On your journey, voyage to the many isles and islands of the sea of thieves digging up treasure
as you go until you are able to take home a horde of cursed chests and precious cargo at the end.
ALL YOURS!!! As well as this, you will learn valuyable combat skills and be educated on how to survive
on a ship for months at a time (dont worry, your tour is only a few hours). Overall, as Ramesy says
"a true pirate is born on the seas, hunting ghosts on islands and fighting to protect his crew."

Choose Flameheart!

Finally, when choosing which ghost tour you truly want, remember that flameheart is just better!
Also, as punishment for not choosing flameheart, you will die. He will hunt you and no matter if
you run, he will find you, and he will kill you. Anyway, BYE!
