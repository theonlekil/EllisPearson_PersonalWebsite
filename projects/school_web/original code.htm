More about STRS

We are a selective grammar school, taking 150 boys into year 7 who join us from Gloucester, the country and
beyond. We have a mixed Sixth Form into which we have welcomed a current total of 150 girls.

In every Ofsted inspection we have been judged Outstanding. We were awarded Specialist College status in
three areas (Languages, Science, Leading Edge Mentor School) and on 1st October 2010 we became one of the
first of the new Academies.
